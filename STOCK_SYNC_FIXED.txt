╔══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╗
║                      🔄 HOW SYNC WORKS                                   ║
╚══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╝

Employee adjusts stock in hostel
         ↓
Database updates hostel_stock table
         ↓
Trigger calculates total: SUM(all hostel stocks)
         ↓
Updates products.stock_quantity
         ↓
Modal reloads product data
         ↓
UI shows updated stock
         ↓
Main website sees updated stock
